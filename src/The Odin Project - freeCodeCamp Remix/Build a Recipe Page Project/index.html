Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

This creamy chocolate fudge is a delightful treat for chocolate lovers. It's rich, smooth, and perfect for any occasion.

With just a few ingredients, you can create this indulgent dessert in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix sugar and cocoa powder in a saucepan.
  2. Add butter and milk, then heat until the mixture boils.
  3. Stir continuously for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and add vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into a greased pan and let it cool.
