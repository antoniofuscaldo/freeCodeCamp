Steve Jobs

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1976 - Co-founds Apple Computer with Steve Wozniak
  • 1984 - Introduces Macintosh revolutionizing personal computing
  • 1985 - Leaves Apple and founds NeXT Computer
  • 1986 - Acquires Pixar Animation Studios
  • 1997 - Returns to Apple as CEO
  • 2001 - Introduces iPod changing digital music industry
  • 2007 - Unveils iPhone launching smartphone revolution
  • 2011 - Passes away at age 56 after long battle with cancer
